# Enterprise CRM System – Architecture & Module Specification

**Document Purpose:**
This document defines the full functional architecture, core modules, workflows, and system blueprint for an industry‑grade CRM platform built around marketing automation, task intelligence, and revenue operations.

---

# 1. Executive Summary

A modern CRM is no longer a simple contact database. It is a **customer intelligence and revenue automation platform** that integrates data, automation, communication, analytics, and AI to drive growth, efficiency, and retention.

This CRM system is designed to:

- Centralize customer data
- Automate marketing and sales workflows
- Drive task-based operational execution
- Optimize revenue pipelines
- Improve customer retention
- Provide enterprise-grade analytics and intelligence

---

# 2. System Design Principles

- Automation-first architecture
- Event-driven workflow orchestration
- Task-based execution model
- Unified customer identity
- Omnichannel engagement
- AI-driven optimization
- Enterprise-grade security and compliance

---

# 3. High-Level System Architecture

```
Frontend Channels → Data Capture → CDP → Workflow Engine → Task Engine →
Sales CRM → Communication Hub → Analytics & AI → Optimization
```

---

# 4. Core Platform Pillars

The CRM system is structured around **8 core pillars**:

1. Identity & Customer Data Platform (CDP)
2. Marketing Automation Engine
3. Task & Workflow Automation Engine
4. Sales CRM & Revenue Pipeline
5. Customer Success & Support System
6. Omnichannel Communication Hub
7. Analytics, BI & AI Intelligence Layer
8. Platform, Security & DevOps Layer

---

**Purpose:** Provide a unified 360° customer view.

### Modules:
- Contact Management
- Account / Company Management
- Identity Resolution Engine
- Customer Profiles
- Behavioral Event Tracking
- Tagging & Segmentation
- Consent & Privacy Management

### Capabilities:
- Unified customer identity
- Data normalization
- Event timeline tracking
- GDPR / compliance-ready consent management

---

## 5.2 Marketing Automation Engine

- Drip Campaign Engine
